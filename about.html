Who I am

I like programming, music of (almost) every type, playing Futsal, long walks, and hanging with my friends. I'm a happy, sometimes shy, other times crazy computer geek.

I'm not a sports adicted but I really like to play futsal with my friends.

Outside of the field I like to watch Soccer, specially Sport Lisboa e Benfica.

In my free time I like to watch some cool TV Series and some action films.
